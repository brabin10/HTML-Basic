All About Frogs

Frogs are amphibians that can be found all over the world. They can be found in habitats ranging from rainforests to deserts or mountains. You have probably seen frogs or toads, the warty kinds of frogs, somewhere around your house or neighborhood.

Different types of frog species

  • Poison Dart Frog
  • Goliath Frog
  • Darwin's Frog

Top five facts about frogs.

  1. A group of frogs is called an army
  2. Frogs drink water through their skin
  3. Frogs are found all over the world
  4. The world’s largest frog species is known as the ‘Goliath Frog’
  5. A frog’s eyes and nose are on the very top of their heads
